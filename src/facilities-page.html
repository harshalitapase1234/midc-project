Facilities

The MIDC Industries Association's office is located in MIDC area in Hingna, Nagpur. MIA provides a lot of facilities to its members out of which a few are mentioned below:

  • Test Lab:
  • To provide testing facilities as per the Standard Of National Test House [Destructive and Non-destructive test of ferrous and on-ferrous metals].
  • Calibration of Engineering Equipments.
  • Testing of rubber, paper, plastic cables, transformers, oil and food products.
  • Auditorium [Capacity 300 persons]
  • A/C Board Room [Capacity 40 persons]
  • Cafeteria
  • Business Center
